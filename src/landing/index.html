Questions

Frequently asked questions

Does Hope AI only work for dental implants?
No. Hope AI was built by implant marketing specialists, so implants are our deepest area of expertise. But the system works for any high-value treatment where patients leave without scheduling. Invisalign, full arch, cosmetic cases, sleep apnea, full mouth rehabilitation: if your TC is presenting it and patients are walking out unconverted, Hope AI recovers them.
What if our practice does a mix of treatments?
That's exactly who Hope AI is built for. Most practices aren't implant-only or Invisalign-only. They're presenting a full mix of high-value cases every week, and Hope AI handles all of it from one platform. Each consult is analyzed based on its specific treatment type, and follow-up sequences are written accordingly.
How does the AI know what kind of treatment was discussed?
Your TC selects the treatment type when they start a recording. The AI then adapts its entire analysis, including objection categories, follow-up tone, and message content, to that specific treatment. An implant follow-up sounds different from an Invisalign follow-up, and Hope AI knows the difference.
Do you connect to our PMS?
Yes. Hope AI integrates with major dental practice management systems to pull actual treatment plan values, appointment data, and patient records. When your PMS is connected, production recovered figures reflect real treatment plan values, not estimates.
